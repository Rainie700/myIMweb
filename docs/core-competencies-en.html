Information Technology Application Ability

Students will grasp the basic principles of information technology and be able to apply it to solve real-world problems. For example, how to design and implement enterprise-level information systems and optimize them.

Data Analysis and Decision-Making Ability

Students will be able to use data analysis tools to extract valuable information from large amounts of data and provide support for business decision-making.

Interdisciplinary Collaboration Ability

Students will have the ability to collaborate effectively across different fields and be able to understand and solve complex problems across disciplines.

Professional Ethics and Sense of Responsibility

We focus on the ethical education of students throughout their learning process, especially how to protect data privacy, ensure system security, and maintain ethical and legal standards in information management work.
