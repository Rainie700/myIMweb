Degree Defense

Process Overview

  1. Students must first complete the Proposal Defense in the previous semester (or earlier) before applying for the Degree Defense in the current semester.
  2. Complete the Academic Ethics Course and Assessment before applying for the exam.
  3. Register your English Name in the campus administrative system.
  4. Submit the Degree Defense Application Form (Master's Degree, Professional Master’s Degree) and fill out the online application form.
  5. Before the defense:
    1. Upload proof of journal or conference paper submission.

      2. If not yet available, submit a delayed thesis submission application with the advisor’s consent; the latest submission is before leaving school.

    2. Complete the "Thesis Originality Check" before the degree defense, submit the results along with the thesis to the degree defense committee members, and ensure the "Thesis Defense Evaluation Form" indicates "Compliant" after verification.
      • Contact the department office one week before the defense to confirm the collection of committee fees and receipts.
      • Conduct the defense (submit the evaluation form and receipts to the department office on the day of the defense).
      • Complete the defense feedback revisions, thesis submission, and originality check system before processing the departure procedure.

Related link: Click to jump to Degree Defense Section

Detailed Instructions

During the thesis writing process, students first complete the "Proposal Defense" and then proceed to the "Degree Defense" in the subsequent semester.

  1. Register English Name: Log in to the campus system and register your English name according to your passport spelling to ensure the accuracy of your English diploma. Detailed steps can be found on the official website.
  2. Complete Academic Ethics Course: Log in to the Taiwan Academic Ethics Education Resource Center and follow the instructions to complete the course and download the certificate for submission.
  3. Apply for Degree Defense: Complete the application form and submit it to the department office by 113/11/22 (Fri).
  4. Thesis Originality Check System: Use the VeriGuide System for the originality check.
  5. Defense Fee Collection: Remind the department office one week in advance about the defense schedule to prepare the fees and receipts.
  6. Prepare Related Forms: Bring necessary forms to the defense day, including evaluation forms and receipts, and submit them afterward.
  7. Cancel Degree Defense: If canceling, complete the cancellation form and submit it to the department office before the defense date.
  8. Departure Procedure: Finish the departure procedure by 114/1/31 (Fri). If unable to complete it on time, submit a "Thesis Submission Extension Report" to the department office by 114/1/24 (Fri).

Graduation Clearance Instructions

Graduate students who have completed their degree examination may apply for graduation clearance between June 24, 2024, and August 31, 2024.

Before Graduation Clearance

  1. Thesis Revision: Complete revisions approved by your advisor and obtain the approval form.
  2. Thesis Printing and Submission:
    1. The approval form must be placed on the first page of the printed thesis.
    2. Thesis upload process: The library review takes about 3 days (contact extension: 82266).
  3. Thesis [Title in Chinese/English] and [Proof of Submission] (mandatory for master’s students)
    • The Chinese and English titles of the thesis must exactly match the "Oral Examination Report Form" (including punctuation, half/full-width characters, and letter case) to ensure consistency on transcripts.
    • Proof of thesis submission: If the proof cannot be uploaded on the day of clearance due to a future publication date, ensure the documents are later submitted to the department office email (im@mail.shu.edu.tw).
  4. Complete the Google Clearance Appointment Form (appointment for the clearance date is required).
  5. Finish the Graduate Destination Survey (contact extension: 82583).

On the Day of Graduation Clearance

Please download the Graduation Clearance Checklist for Information Management Graduate Students. Ensure it is signed by your advisor and the approval form is obtained.

  1. Collect the clearance process form from the Office of Academic Affairs (proceed to all required units).
  2. Visit the department office to:
    1. Verify the [Thesis Title in Chinese/English].
    2. Verify the [Proof of Thesis Submission].
    3. Submit the [Graduation Clearance Checklist for Information Management Graduate Students].
  3. At the Office of Academic Affairs, bring your [Student ID] and [Thesis] for verification (ensure thesis title accuracy).
  4. At the library, submit the printed thesis:
    1. Submit one copy if the electronic full-text file is available immediately.
    2. Submit two copies if the electronic full-text file is set for delayed release.
  5. If you cannot personally complete the process, prepare an authorization letter and the following documents:
    1. Authorization letter, student ID, and personal seal of the authorizing student.
    2. Original ID cards of both the authorizing student and the authorized representative.
    3. If the clearance process is incomplete, the authorized representative must finish the process on behalf of the student before collecting the diploma.
  6. Processing time from June 24 to August 31 on weekdays:
    Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM–12:00 PM and 1:30 PM–4:30 PM

    7. (Graduate students with approved deferred submission may complete clearance from August 1 to August 31. Students who fail to complete clearance must register for the second semester of the 112th academic year. Those whose study period has expired should follow the regulations on degree examination for subsequent registration procedures.)
