2024 Annual Alumni Gathering

Dear Information Management Alumni,

Shih Hsin University's Department of Information Management has been established for over 30 years, achieving remarkable accomplishments across various fields. To strengthen the bond between our faculty, students, and alumni, and to enhance interactions, we are pleased to announce the "Alumni Homecoming Event" on Thursday, November 21, 2024, at 7:00 PM, to be held on the 2nd floor of the College of Management. This event aims to foster connections among graduates, provide a platform for resource integration between the department and alumni, and serve as a bridge for industry-academia collaboration. It also embodies the mission of Shih Hsin Information Management to pass down valuable experiences and promote departmental development. We sincerely invite you to join us and make this occasion a grand success.

Event Details

Time: Thursday, November 21, 2024, at 7:00 PM

Location: 2nd Floor, College of Management

Agenda: The event includes a dinner, alumni speeches, and a presentation on departmental development. Alumni from all graduating classes are welcome!
