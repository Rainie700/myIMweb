Master's Program Admission Information

In the rapidly changing era of digital technology and AI, the global demand for information talents is surging. Want to become job-ready? No matter your background, apply to the Shih Hsin University Master’s Program in Information Management and become a sought-after professional in the workforce!

Program Introduction

The Master’s Program in Information Management at Shih Hsin University aims to cultivate professionals with innovative thinking and practical skills in information technology management. The curriculum covers business innovation and management capabilities in the AI era, cloud service applications and management, as well as data analysis and communication skills. It particularly emphasizes the practical application of IT in businesses to solve problems and enhance competitiveness. In addition, students can choose cross-disciplinary courses from four major colleges: Management, Communication, Humanities and Social Sciences, and Law. Whether or not you have an IT background, the Master’s Program offers suitable learning pathways. The flexibility of diverse electives and the added value of industry-academia integration will help you stand out and thrive in the AI era.

Welcome Recent Graduates

Shih Hsin Information Management warmly welcomes recent graduates to further their studies. Regardless of whether you majored in Information Management during your undergraduate studies, as long as you are interested in deeper learning in the field, the Master’s Program is an ideal platform for your professional advancement. We also invite your friends and family to join the Shih Hsin Information Management family. By pursuing the Master’s Program, you can enhance your professional skills and significantly boost your career prospects.
