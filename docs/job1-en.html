Position: PR Consultant

Full-time PR Consultant:

  • Eligibility: Fresh graduates or individuals with less than three years of experience
  • Salary: Starting at NT$45,000, with an annual salary of approximately NT$600,000-650,000 (including bonuses)
  • Application Deadline: May 15, 2024

Contract-based PR Consultant:

  • Eligibility: Third-year undergraduates or graduate students
  • Compensation: NT$200 per hour
  • Work Schedule: Full-time during summer (Monday to Friday); at least three working days per week during the semester
  • Contract Period: 3-6 months
  • Reporting Dates: Every July, October, and March

Application Process:

Please submit your resume via the 104 Recruitment Page.
