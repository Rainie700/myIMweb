Master's In-Service Program Admission Information

In the rapidly changing era of digital technology and AI, global demand for information professionals is surging. Want to become job-ready? Regardless of your field of expertise, apply to the Shih Hsin University Master’s Program in Information Management to become a highly sought-after talent in the workplace.

Features of the Master's In-Service Program

The Master's In-Service Program in Information Management at Shih Hsin University is dedicated to cultivating professionals with both information and management capabilities. The curriculum combines theory and practice, providing working professionals with opportunities to further their education, stay abreast of the latest trends in information technology, and adapt to the changes of the AI era. Shih Hsin University’s Information Management Graduate Institute (EMBA) aims to nurture professionals with innovative thinking and practical skills in IT management. The program is tailored for working individuals, covering business innovation and management capabilities in the AI era, cloud service applications and management, and data analysis and communication skills. It emphasizes the practical application of IT to solve business problems and enhance competitiveness. Additionally, students can choose cross-disciplinary courses from four major colleges: Management, Communication, Humanities and Social Sciences, and Law. Whether or not you have an IT background, you will find a suitable learning path in the Master’s Program. Flexible elective options allow you to thrive and stand out in the AI era. Shih Hsin warmly welcomes alumni to return and pursue further studies. Whether you have already achieved certain accomplishments in your career or wish to deepen your learning in information management, the Master’s In-Service Program provides an ideal platform for you to achieve the next peak in your career. We also invite your friends and family to join the Shih Hsin Information Management family. Through the Master’s In-Service Program, you can learn alongside professionals from various industries, expand your network, enhance your skills, and significantly boost your career value.

Application and Screening Information

Screening Items: Document Review

  • Autobiography and Proof of Work Experience【60%】
  • Study Plan and Other Supporting Documents【40%】

Application Period, Fees, and Submission Deadline: 2024/10/14–2024/11/29

Application Process: Click here to access the application link → Select Applicant → Download Adobe software → Enter application details

Program Code: 660

Department: Master’s In-Service Program in Information Management

Admission Quota: 23

Eligibility:

  • Education: Bachelor’s degree or equivalent, meeting the Ministry of Education’s requirements for applying to a master’s program.
  • Experience: Individuals currently or previously employed with at least one year of cumulative work experience. Work experience is calculated up to September 30, 2024. Part-time work experience may also be included.
  • Military service (mandatory or substitute service) can be counted toward work experience.

Required Documents for Review:

  • Autobiography (including study motivation and goals)
  • Study Plan
  • Proof of Work Experience (use the form provided in Appendix 1 of this brochure or submit your own)
  • Other supporting documents (e.g., professional certifications, outstanding achievements in work-related fields)

Screening Items: Document Review

Grading Criteria:

  • Autobiography and Proof of Work Experience【60%】
  • Study Plan and Other Supporting Documents【40%】

Tiebreaker Criteria:

  1. Study Plan and Other Supporting Documents
  2. Autobiography and Proof of Work Experience
  3. Quota includes three additional slots for the semiconductor, AI, and mechanical fields.
