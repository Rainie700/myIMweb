Course Content

The senior year curriculum focuses on integrating various information skills and applying them comprehensively through internships or projects.

Required Courses

Graduation Project (II)

Course Description: Students conduct a complete research or practical project, from planning to completion, showcasing the professional knowledge and application skills acquired during their studies.

(Fall/Spring) Credits: (3/0)

Elective Courses

Strategic Management

Course Description: Explores how to formulate and execute business strategies by analyzing internal and external environments to achieve organizational goals, with an emphasis on practical applications.

(Fall/Spring) Credits: (3/0)

Special Research Topics

Course Description: Students select a specific topic for in-depth research and reporting, cultivating independent thinking and research abilities.

(Fall/Spring) Credits: (2/0)

Practical Management of Graduation Projects

Course Description: Teaches how to plan and manage graduation projects, covering project management and time control to enhance execution capabilities.

(Fall/Spring) Credits: (2/0)

Enterprise Resource Planning Programming

Course Description: Introduces the design and development of enterprise resource planning systems, teaching students how to write programs to support ERP system functions.

(Fall/Spring) Credits: (3/0)

Applications of Data Science

Course Description: Explores the use of data science techniques to analyze and solve real-world problems, covering data processing, modeling, and visualization.

(Fall/Spring) Credits: (3/0)

Innovation, Creativity, and Entrepreneurship

Course Description: Students will learn how to transform creative ideas into business opportunities, exploring entrepreneurial processes and innovative thinking.

(Fall/Spring) Credits: (2/0)

Swift Programming

Course Description: Teaches the fundamentals and applications of the Swift programming language, enabling students to develop iOS applications and understand its development framework.

(Fall/Spring) Credits: (3/0)
