Overseas Internship Application Details

Home | Chinese

Application Period:

Mainland China: From now until November 29, 2024.

Application Process:

Internships in Mainland China:

  1. Complete the "Shih Hsin University Overseas Internship Application Form (including Internship Code of Conduct Agreement)" and submit it to the department office before 11/29. Click here for the file
  2. For those who already have a resume, send it to pntlight@mail.shu.edu.tw
  3. For those who have not prepared a resume, go to the "Industry-Academia Internship Matching Platform" to complete a personal resume and submit it online. Click here for platform usage instructions

Provided Benefits:

  • Monthly internship allowance of 3,500-4,000 RMB
  • Accommodation provided by the company (utility costs such as water, electricity, and heating to be borne by the user)
  • Upon completing the internship, a flight subsidy of 2,000-2,500 RMB will be provided; Japanese companies will provide a one-way ticket for the outbound journey, and the return ticket will be provided after passing course evaluations.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Only seniors or extended study students are eligible to apply
  • Upon acceptance, applicants must enroll in the courses "Overseas Workplace Internship," "Advanced Overseas Internship," or "Overseas Internship Project," which count for 9 credits in the second semester of their senior year. (These cannot replace failed courses from freshman to the first semester of senior year; credit replacement eligibility depends on departmental regulations.)
  • Priority will be given to students who have completed their credits in advance and met graduation requirements (e.g., English proficiency tests).
  • The overseas internship is a formal course, including pre-, during-, and post-internship guidance, and requires full tuition payment.
