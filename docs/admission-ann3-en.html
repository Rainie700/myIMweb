2025 Academic Year Master’s Program for Working Professionals Admission

In the rapidly evolving digital and AI era, the demand for global information technology professionals is skyrocketing. Want to become workplace-ready? No matter your field, enroll in the Shih Hsin University Master’s Program in Information Management to become a highly sought-after professional.

Screening Items: Document Review

  • Autobiography and Proof of Work Experience [60%]
  • Study Plan and Other Supporting Documents [40%]

Application, Fees, and Submission Deadline: 2024/10/14 ~ 2024/11/29

Application Process: Click here to apply → Select "Applicant" → Download Adobe Software → Enter Application Details

Department Code: 660

Program Name: Master’s Program for Working Professionals, Department of Information Management

Admission Quota: 23 students

Eligibility:

  • Education: University graduate or equivalent, meeting the Ministry of Education’s requirements for Master’s program applicants.
  • Experience: Current or former employees with at least one year of cumulative work experience. Work experience calculation is as of September 30, 2024. Part-time work can be included in the calculation.
  • Military service (mandatory or substitute) can also be counted as work experience.

Department-Specified Review Documents:

  • Autobiography (including study motivation and goals)
  • Study Plan
  • Proof of Work Experience (use the provided template or self-issue)
  • Other Supporting Documents (e.g., professional certifications, achievements in the field, etc.)

Screening Items: Document Review

Score Calculation Method:

  • Autobiography and Proof of Work Experience [60%]
  • Study Plan and Other Supporting Documents [40%]

Tiebreaker Criteria:

  1. Study Plan and Other Supporting Documents
  2. Autobiography and Proof of Work Experience
  3. Admission quota includes 3 additional seats for Semiconductor, AI, and Mechanical Engineering fields.
