Department's Vision and Goals for the Future

With the rapid development of information technology, our department will continue to adjust its curriculum design to meet industry demands and guide students in facing future challenges. Our goal is to cultivate information management professionals who possess an innovative spirit and the ability to collaborate across disciplines.

Curriculum Innovation and Updates

Our department will continuously introduce the latest technologies and research findings. In the future, we will offer courses related to artificial intelligence, cloud computing, the Internet of Things, and other fields, so that students can grasp future technological trends.

Strengthening International Cooperation

To enhance students' international competitiveness, we will strengthen cooperation with international academic institutions, offering more exchange programs and overseas internship opportunities to broaden students' global perspectives.

Ongoing Research and Innovation

The department will continue to increase investment in academic research, particularly in areas such as data science, big data, and cloud technologies, while actively participating in international academic exchanges and collaborations.
