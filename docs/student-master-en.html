Internship Process

Expected internship period: February 19, 2024, to May 31, 2024; approximately 3.5 months; duration flexible upon discussion.

Steps to Complete Before the Internship

  1. Fill out the corporate internship form Form Link
  2. Join the Industrial Practice Course Group Group Link
  3. Confirm the internship contract
    1. Download the internship contract template for company confirmation. If there are any modifications, the company should contact the department office or Professor Chen Yu-Liang.
    2. The internship contract must be a tripartite agreement (three copies). Once the company and department confirm the contract, the company should stamp the contract before sending it to the department for stamping. The student should then return the contract to the company.
  4. Make sure to register for the "Industrial Practice" course (3 credits).

    5. Course Notes:

    1. This course requires finding an internship company! (If you have already registered but are sure you don't have a company, please drop the course, or you'll receive no grade!)
    2. The internship must take place during the course period. The internship company must either be announced by the department or self-sourced, with approval and a signed internship contract.
    3. Attendance is mandatory for the first class.
